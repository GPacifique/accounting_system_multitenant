### **File Formats Required**

### **Size Variations Needed**

#### **Full Logo** (Horizontal Layout)
- **Dimensions**: Approximately 3:1 ratio (width to height)
- **Minimum Size**: 120px wide for web use
- **Usage**: Headers, letterheads, marketing materials

#### **Compact Logo** (Square/Vertical Layout)
- **Dimensions**: 1:1 or 4:3 ratio
- **Minimum Size**: 40px for web use
- **Usage**: Sidebar, mobile navigation, social media

#### **Icon Version** (Symbol Only)
- **Dimensions**: Perfect square (1:1 ratio)
- **Minimum Size**: 16px (favicon)
- **Maximum Size**: 512px (high-resolution displays)
- **Usage**: Favicons, app icons, social media profile pictures
